How BOLO works

You start with a Book Brief — not a collection of disconnected prompts.

Describe the topic, target reader, knowledge level, purpose and tone. BOLO uses your brief to create a complete, coherent piece of writing — a book or educational material, with or without illustrations.

When generation is complete, you have a complete, coherent, publication-ready book or educational text as an editable DOCX, print-ready PDF and EPUB. Before commercial publication, you can use Quality Check for an additional fact review. With Edit, you can, for example, refine the tone of a chapter or the text under a subheading with a simple instruction. The DOCX file can also always be edited manually.

You choose between regular Dictate and Clean Dictate. With Clean Dictate turned on, you receive the cleaned-up text directly while the regular dictation is saved as a backup. The cleaned-up version preserves names, places and other important details.
