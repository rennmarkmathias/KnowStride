Quick introduction

BOLO is desktop software for structured AI-assisted book production.

It is designed for nonfiction and fiction books, guides, manuals, educational material and other knowledge-based content.

Instead of working with isolated prompts, BOLO guides the full publishing workflow: from a book brief to outline, chapter generation, editing, illustrations, cover creation and export.

For illustrations, BOLO can suggest image ideas from the manuscript, generate the images, place them in the book and add captions automatically. You choose the number of images, review the suggestions, click generate and then export.

BOLO can also create a full book cover, translate finished books and export the result as EPUB and print-ready DOCX and PDF.
