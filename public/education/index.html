Quality control: not just “AI text”

BOLO is built as a production system, not as a simple prompt box. Several layers help turn the brief into a consistent and usable result, so the output does not become loose, generic AI text.

Structure engine The material is built around chapters, headings and content flow instead of separate chat responses.
Volume and balance control BOLO helps keep text length, chapter scope and information level balanced.
Terminology and factual consistency Concepts, names, facts and tone are kept consistent throughout the material.
Export and editing workflow The result can be exported to DOCX, PDF or EPUB. Because DOCX is editable, the teacher can add a separate page with instructions, notes or comments before the final PDF export.
