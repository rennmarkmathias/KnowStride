Quick Start

  1. Enter Book Information (title, subtitle, author).
  2. Create or paste a Book Brief.
  3. Choose structure mode: AutoBook, AI Sections or Manual Outline.
  4. Adjust settings in Book Size & Length and Writing Style & AI Control.
  5. Run Test Mode first, for example 2-3 chapters.
  6. Generate the full book when the test result looks correct.
Tip - Read through the guide before starting. It saves time and makes testing more consistent.

■System Requirements

Tip: If you are new, start with a small test project (1–3 chapters) to understand the workflow.

OpenAI API key: Go to https://platform.openai.com/api-keys, sign in if needed (you can use Google, Apple, Microsoft or email), click Create new secret key and save it (you will only see it once), open Billing in the left menu and add a small amount (for example $5 to start), then paste the key into BOLO and start generating.

BOLO is a Windows desktop application and requires Windows 10 or Windows 11.

You also need Microsoft Word or LibreOffice installed, plus your own OpenAI API key.

DOCX manuscripts are usually opened in Word or LibreOffice for review and editing after generation.

LibreOffice can be downloaded free of charge from the official website.

■1. Introduction

BOLO is designed for AI-assisted creation of books, especially nonfiction, guidebooks and research-based projects.

An experimental Fiction Mode also exists, but the system is primarily optimized for nonfiction workflows.

■2. When to Use BOLO

BOLO is usually used after the concept and research phase is complete. The most important preparation step is a clear and detailed Book Brief.

The software is then used to generate the outline, draft the manuscript, export files and produce the cover.

■4. Recommended Workflow for Creating a Book

The most effective workflow usually starts with a detailed Book Brief. A longer and more specific brief generally gives better results.
Note: The Base Prompt is only used to create your Book Brief. Do not paste it directly into BOLO.

A common workflow is:

1. Click Copy Base Prompt in BOLO.
2. Paste the prompt into an AI service such as ChatGPT.
3. Develop the book idea into a clear and detailed Book Brief.
4. Refine the brief through a few iterations until the structure feels right.
5. Paste the finished brief into BOLO.
6. Choose how to create your outline:
- AutoBook: generate a full outline from your Book Brief
- AI Sections: use your own chapter titles and let BOLO create subheadings
- Manual Outline: use your own complete outline (chapters and subheadings)
7. Run Test Mode before generating the full manuscript.
Tip: In Test Mode, compare the estimated page count with your target (for example 120 vs 200 pages). If the estimate is significantly lower, adjust your Book Brief by expanding the content or splitting topics into more chapters. This is most relevant for narrower subjects.
8. Review and edit the DOCX manuscript if needed.
9. Use Re-Export when you need a repaired or refreshed export after external editing.

Manual Outline format example:

- Subheading
- Subheading
- Subheading

Next Chapter Title
- Subheading
- Subheading

For narrow subjects, be explicit about what each chapter should contain. For very broad subjects, keep expectations realistic in relation to the selected page count. You cannot expect a full world history in 50 pages, and you should not expect a 500-page book about a small town of 10,000 inhabitants.

■5. Cost

A rough guideline is that a page of approximately 300 words costs around $0.01 to generate.

Typical costs are around $1–4 for nonfiction books. Fiction books can cost 3–10x more depending on complexity and narrative depth.

It is recommended to use Test Mode before generating a full manuscript.

■6. Cover Generator - Recommended Workflow

Cover Generator creates a complete cover layout. Upload the DOCX manuscript, a front cover image and a back cover image.

The program calculates the spine automatically based on the manuscript length. You can then adjust Spine Position, title color, subtitle color, author color and spine color.

It is recommended to make a few test exports before final production.

The program automatically generates cover text. You can use it as is or shorten it manually or with AI tools like ChatGPT.
You can also generate front and back cover images using AI tools. A resolution of 300 DPI is recommended for print.

If the cover is converted to PDF, do not open it immediately. Wait about 20 seconds for the file to be fully processed.

Key settings
Book Size & Length, Writing Style & AI Control, Units and Anchor Data Density affect both the content and the final format of the book.
Good practice
Small changes in instruction strength, brief quality and Test Mode are often the fastest way to compare output quality before committing to a full run.

■7. Functions and Settings Explained

Book Information contains metadata such as title, subtitle and author.

Book Brief is the main instruction used by the AI and is the most important input in the workflow.

Copy Base Prompt copies a prompt template that can be used in ChatGPT or another AI tool to build a strong brief.

Save Setup stores your current settings so you can continue later without rebuilding the project.

AutoBook generates a complete outline automatically.

AI Sections creates subheadings when you already have chapter titles.

Manual Outline gives you full manual control of the structure.

Book Size & Length defines the number of volumes and pages per volume.

Writing Style & AI Control influences tone, style and how closely the AI follows the instructions.

Units switches between metric and imperial systems.

Anchor Data Density controls how many concrete facts, figures and data points appear in the text.

Test Mode generates a limited number of chapters for evaluation before a full run.

Fiction Mode: When enabled, only Book Size & Length is relevant in Publishing Setup. Writing Style, AI Control and Smart Enhancement have limited effect in this mode.

■8. Export and Re-Export

After a full generation, BOLO can produce DOCX, EPUB and, when needed, PRINT PDF.

DOCX is the main working format. EPUB is intended for e-book publishing. PRINT PDF is mainly used for print workflows and is therefore not always needed in the first step.

If you edit the manuscript in Microsoft Word, LibreOffice or another editor, the document structure may change. Save the file again as .docx and run Re-Export in BOLO to rebuild the internal structure and improve compatibility with publishing platforms such as Amazon KDP.

Note (LibreOffice / OpenOffice): If you do not use Microsoft Word, you can still edit the manuscript. Open the DOCX file in LibreOffice or a similar tool, make your changes, then save the file and ensure it is saved as .docx. After that, use Re-Export in BOLO to repair and rebuild the document structure.

■9. Presentation

Generate Presentation is an additional tool and not the primary purpose of the software. It creates presentation material based on the Brief.

Presentation includes two modes:

Standard - generates a presentation directly from the Brief.

Website Overview - allows you to generate a presentation from a website.
1. Open Generate Presentation
2. Start Presentation Mode
3. Select Website Overview
4. Enter the website URL
5. Click Generate

Important: For both Presentation and Flyer, make sure to select the correct language in the Generate menu before starting.

■10. Flyer

Generate Flyer creates a short informational sheet based on the Brief.

The flyer can be used for quick summaries, project descriptions or simple promotional material.

  • Company / service flyer – like a simple company information sheet.

  • Destination / place flyer – like a tourist leaflet or place overview.
  • Educational / informational flyer – like a museum handout, school sheet or public information page.
Practical advice: If you want the result to feel like a normal book, start with A5, 6 × 9 in or Royal. If you want more space for visuals, tables or workbook-style layouts, choose B5, 7 × 10 in, A4 or 8.5 × 11 in.

■4. Anbefalet workflow til at skabe en bog

Det mest effektive workflow starter normalt med en detaljeret Book Brief. En længere og mere specifik brief giver normalt bedre resultater.
Bemærk: Base Prompt bruges kun til at skabe din Book Brief. Indsæt den ikke direkte i BOLO.

Et almindeligt workflow er:

1. Klik på Copy Base Prompt i BOLO.
2. Indsæt prompten i en AI-tjeneste som ChatGPT.
3. Udvikl bogideen til en klar og detaljeret Book Brief.
4. Forfin briefen gennem nogle iterationer, indtil strukturen føles rigtig.
5. Indsæt den færdige brief i BOLO.

- AI Sections: brug dine egne kapiteltitler og lad BOLO lave underoverskrifter

9. Brug Re-Export, når du skal reparere eller genopbygge eksporten efter ekstern redigering.

■8. Eksport og Re-Export

Efter en fuld generering kan BOLO producere DOCX, EPUB og ved behov PRINT PDF.

DOCX er det vigtigste arbejdsformat. EPUB er beregnet til e-bogsudgivelse. PRINT PDF bruges primært til tryk og er derfor ikke altid nødvendigt i første trin.

Hvis du redigerer manuskriptet i Microsoft Word, LibreOffice eller en anden editor, kan dokumentstrukturen ændre sig. Gem filen igen som .docx og kør Re-Export i BOLO for at genopbygge den interne struktur og forbedre kompatibiliteten med platforme som Amazon KDP.

Bemærk (LibreOffice / OpenOffice): Hvis du ikke bruger Microsoft Word, kan du stadig redigere manuskriptet. Åbn DOCX-filen i LibreOffice, OpenOffice eller et lignende værktøj, lav dine ændringer, og sørg for, at filen gemmes i .docx-format. Brug derefter Re-Export i BOLO til at reparere og genopbygge dokumentstrukturen.

■8. Export och Re-Export

Efter en full generering kan BOLO skapa DOCX, EPUB och vid behov PRINT PDF.

DOCX är det huvudsakliga arbetsformatet. EPUB är avsett för e-bokspublicering. PRINT PDF används främst för tryck och behövs därför inte alltid i första steget.

Om du redigerar manuset i Microsoft Word, LibreOffice eller ett annat program kan dokumentstrukturen förändras. Spara filen igen som .docx och kör Re-Export i BOLO för att bygga om den interna strukturen och förbättra kompatibiliteten med plattformar som Amazon KDP.

Obs (LibreOffice / OpenOffice): Om du inte använder Microsoft Word kan du ändå redigera manuset. Öppna DOCX-filen i LibreOffice, OpenOffice eller ett liknande verktyg, gör dina ändringar och se till att filen sparas i .docx-format. Använd sedan Re-Export i BOLO för att reparera och bygga om dokumentstrukturen.

■12. Format och typiska användningsområden

Det här avsnittet är tänkt som en praktisk referens. Programmet visar exakta storlekar i millimeter och inches, men många användare förstår formaten snabbare när de jämförs med saker de redan känner igen, som en pocketbok, en vanlig nonfiction-bok, en arbetsbok eller en coffee table-bok.

BOLO innehåller följande bokformat: A5, A4, B5, Royal, Crown, Pocket, 152 × 229 mm (6 × 9 in), 127 × 203 mm (5 × 8 in), 178 × 254 mm (7 × 10 in) och 216 × 279 mm (8.5 × 11 in). Programmet innehåller också Standard presentation och Website overview i presentationsverktyget samt flyergenerering som anpassar sig till olika flyertyper.

  • 6 × 9 in är en av de vanligaste standardstorlekarna för nonfiction-böcker.

A4 · 210 × 297 mm · 216 × 279 mm (8.5 × 11 in)
Exempeltitel

  • Dessa format känns mer som dokument eller pärmar än som en traditionell bok från bokhandeln.

■Enkel formatlogik

Tänk “Pocket”

Liten, kompakt och lätt att hålla. Bra när du vill ha en riktig pocketkänsla.

Tänk “Normal bok”

A5, 6 × 9 och Royal är de säkraste valen för vanliga nonfiction-böcker.

Tänk “Arbetsbok / rapport”

B5, 7 × 10, A4 och 8.5 × 11 är bäst när layoutyta betyder mer än bokhandelskänsla.

■Presentationsformat

BOLO har två huvudlägen för presentationer:

  • Standard presentation – bygger presentationen direkt från din Brief.
  • Website overview – skapar en presentation från en webbadress i ett strukturerat format.

I praktiken kan du tänka på det som skillnaden mellan “gör slides om mitt ämne” och “sammanfatta den här webbplatsen som en presentation”.

■Flyerformat

Flyerverktyget skapar ett informationsblad på en sida. I praktiken kan det kännas som någon av dessa välkända typer:

  • Company / service flyer – som ett enkelt informationsblad om företag eller tjänst.
  • Product flyer – som ett produktblad eller ett säljblad på en sida.
  • Event flyer – liknar en inbjudan, eventflyer eller enkel affischlik informationssida.
  • Destination / place flyer – som ett turistblad eller en platsöversikt.
  • Educational / informational flyer – som ett museiblad, skolblad eller en offentlig informationssida.
Praktiskt råd: Om du vill att resultatet ska kännas som en vanlig bok, börja med A5, 6 × 9 in eller Royal. Om du vill ha mer plats för bilder, tabeller eller arbetsboksliknande layout, välj B5, 7 × 10 in, A4 eller 8.5 × 11 in.
