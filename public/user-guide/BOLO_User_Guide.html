■1. Introduction

BOLO is designed for AI-assisted creation of books, especially nonfiction, guidebooks and research-based projects.

An experimental Fiction Mode also exists, but the system is primarily optimized for nonfiction workflows.

BOLO is usually used after the concept and research phase is complete. The most important preparation step is a clear and detailed Book Brief.

The software is then used to generate the outline, draft the manuscript, illustrate, export files and produce the cover.

The program should normally be used in fullscreen mode. If the window is too small, some controls and buttons may not be visible. You can also zoom the interface with Ctrl + and Ctrl - if needed.

■2. Recommended Workflow for Creating a Book

The most effective workflow usually starts with a detailed Book Brief. A longer and more specific brief generally gives better results.
Note: The Base Prompt is only used to create your Book Brief. Do not paste it directly into BOLO.

A common workflow is:

1. Click Copy Base Prompt in BOLO.
2. Paste the prompt into an AI service such as ChatGPT.
3. Develop the book idea into a clear and detailed Book Brief.
4. Refine the brief through a few iterations until the structure feels right.
5. Paste the finished brief into BOLO.
6. Choose how to create your outline:
- AutoBook: generate a full outline from your Book Brief
- AI Sections: use your own chapter titles and let BOLO create subheadings
- Manual Outline: use your own complete outline (chapters and subheadings)
7. Run Test Mode before generating the full manuscript.
Tip: In Test Mode, compare the estimated page count with your target (for example 120 vs 200 pages). If the estimate is significantly lower, adjust your Book Brief by expanding the content or splitting topics into more chapters. This is most relevant for narrower subjects.
8. Review and edit the DOCX manuscript if needed.
9. Use Re-Export when you need a repaired or refreshed export after external editing.

Manual Outline format example:

Chapter Title
- Subheading
- Subheading
- Subheading

Next Chapter Title
- Subheading
- Subheading

For narrow subjects, be explicit about what each chapter should contain. For very broad subjects, keep expectations realistic in relation to the selected page count. You cannot expect a full world history in 50 pages, and you should not expect a 500-page book about a small town of 10,000 inhabitants.

■3. Cost

A rough guideline is that a page of approximately 300 words costs around $0.01 to generate.

Typical costs are around $1–4 for nonfiction books. Fiction books can cost 3–10x more depending on complexity and narrative depth.

It is recommended to use Test Mode before generating a full manuscript.

Example (Full Book Cost): A 300-page book in standard 6×9 inch format typically costs around $3 to generate.

Image generation costs approximately $0.20 per image. If you want 20 images and regenerate 10 of them, you generate 30 images in total, which costs about $6.

Total example cost: $3 (text) + $6 (images) = approximately $9 for a fully illustrated book.

■4. Illustrate

You can easily add illustrations to your book after generating your manuscript.

The Fastest Way (Recommended)

  1. Go to the Illustrate tab.
  2. Upload your manuscript.
  3. Choose Image size in DOCX.

You can either let BOLO decide the image size automatically (BOLO decides (mixed)), or choose a fixed size for all images.

Available fixed sizes:

  • Thumbnail
  • Small
  • Medium (~45% width)
  • Large

If you choose a fixed size, all images will use that size. If you let BOLO decide, image sizes will be adapted automatically based on context, you can of course change the suggested size in the list M instead of L for example.

  1. Enter the number of suggestions.
  2. Click Suggest Image Placement.
Tip: Request 2–3× more images than you actually need. For example, request 30 suggestions if you want 10 images.
  1. Review the suggestions.
  2. Select the ones you like.
  3. Click Update marker DOCX.

This removes all unused image markers and keeps only the selected ones in your manuscript.

Important: If you skip this step, all suggested markers will remain in the document — including those you did not select — and must be removed manually.
  1. Click Generate Images.

BOLO will automatically create relevant illustrations, place them in your book and add captions.

Option 2: Write Image Instructions

You can manually define images directly in your manuscript.

Use the following format:

(IMAGE: location; year; description)

Example:

(IMAGE: Stockholm waterfront; 1750; Formal quays, warehouses, bridges, and ships at a growing port-capital)

  1. Upload the manuscript in the Illustrate tab.
  2. Click Scan Image Markers.
  3. Generate images.

Option 3: Upload Your Own Images

You can upload your own images with captions and place them in the manuscript.

  1. Scroll to the bottom of the Illustrate tab.
  2. Upload your images.
  3. Add captions.
  4. Place markers in your manuscript: (picture 1), (picture 2), (picture 3).
  5. Click Scan Image Markers.
  6. Generate images.
Important workflow note: Illustrations affect the layout and total page count of your book. For this reason, illustration should be completed before creating the cover, so the final page count and spine width are calculated correctly.

Final Step

Once your images are generated, export your document and review the result.

Note on Table of Contents

Adding images and editing text can affect page layout. Because BOLO allows multiple ways of modifying content — both through editing and image generation — automatically updating the table of contents continuously can become unstable.

For this reason, BOLO uses a static table of contents without page numbers. We recommend adding page numbers manually at the final stage. This only takes a few minutes and ensures full control and accuracy.

■5. Cover Generator - Recommended Workflow

Cover Generator creates a complete cover layout. Upload the DOCX manuscript, a front cover image and a back cover image.

The program calculates the spine automatically based on the manuscript length. You can then adjust Spine Position, title color, subtitle color, author color and spine color.

It is recommended to make a few test exports before final production.

The program automatically generates cover text. You can use it as is or shorten it manually or with AI tools like ChatGPT.
You can also generate front and back cover images using AI tools. A resolution of 300 DPI is recommended for print.

If the cover is converted to PDF, do not open it immediately. Wait about 20 seconds for the file to be fully processed.

■6. Functions and Settings Explained

Book Information contains metadata such as title, subtitle and author.

Copy Base Prompt copies a prompt template that can be used in ChatGPT or another AI tool to build a strong brief.

Test Mode generates a limited number of chapters for evaluation before a full run.

■7. Export and Re-Export

After a full generation, BOLO can produce DOCX, EPUB and, when needed, PRINT PDF.

DOCX is the main working format. EPUB is intended for e-book publishing. PRINT PDF is mainly used for specific print workflows or local printing, while DOCX is commonly used for platforms such as Amazon KDP. It is therefore not always needed in the first step.

If you edit the manuscript in Microsoft Word, LibreOffice or another editor, the document structure may change. Save the file again as .docx and run Re-Export in BOLO to rebuild the internal structure and improve compatibility with publishing platforms such as Amazon KDP.

Note (LibreOffice / OpenOffice): If you do not use Microsoft Word, you can still edit the manuscript. Open the DOCX file in LibreOffice or a similar tool, make your changes, then save the file and ensure it is saved as .docx. After that, use Re-Export in BOLO to repair and rebuild the document structure.

The best way to learn BOLO is through testing and experimentation.

We recommend running small test generations before full production, testing illustrations and image sizes, and trying different cover layouts.

As long as you keep your original generated manuscript file, you cannot break or damage your project. This allows you to test different approaches, regenerate content and images, and create multiple versions.

Running small tests and experimenting with images or layouts is very low cost. This makes it practical to learn the system through real usage.

■7. Eksport og Re-Export

Efter en fuld generering kan BOLO producere DOCX, EPUB og ved behov PRINT PDF.

DOCX er det vigtigste arbejdsformat. EPUB er beregnet til e-bogsudgivelse. PRINT PDF bruges primært til specifikke printworkflows eller lokal udskrivning, mens DOCX ofte bruges til platforme som Amazon KDP. Derfor er det ikke altid nødvendigt i første trin.

Hvis du redigerer manuskriptet i Microsoft Word, LibreOffice eller en anden editor, kan dokumentstrukturen ændre sig. Gem filen igen som .docx og kør Re-Export i BOLO for at genopbygge den interne struktur og forbedre kompatibiliteten med udgivelsesplatforme som Amazon KDP.

Bemærk (LibreOffice / OpenOffice): Hvis du ikke bruger Microsoft Word, kan du stadig redigere manuskriptet. Åbn DOCX-filen i LibreOffice eller et lignende værktøj, lav dine ændringer, gem filen og sørg for, at den er gemt som .docx. Brug derefter Re-Export i BOLO til at reparere og genopbygge dokumentstrukturen.

BOLO är utformat för AI-assisterat skapande av böcker, särskilt sakprosa, guideböcker och forskningsbaserade projekt.

■3. Kostnad

En grov riktlinje är att en sida på cirka 300 ord kostar omkring $0.01 att generera.

Typiska kostnader ligger runt $1–4 för fackböcker och annan sakprosa. Skönlitterära böcker kan kosta 3–10 gånger mer beroende på komplexitet och berättelsedjup.

Det rekommenderas att använda Test Mode innan du genererar ett helt manus.

Exempel (total bokkostnad): En bok på 300 sidor i standardformatet 6×9 tum kostar vanligtvis omkring $3 att generera.

Bildgenerering kostar ungefär $0.20 per bild. Om du vill ha 20 bilder och genererar om 10 av dem, genererar du totalt 30 bilder, vilket kostar cirka $6.

Exempel på total kostnad: $3 (text) + $6 (bilder) = ungefär $9 för en fullt illustrerad bok.

■7. Export och Re-Export

Efter en fullständig generering kan BOLO producera DOCX, EPUB och vid behov PRINT PDF.

DOCX är det viktigaste arbetsformatet. EPUB är avsett för e-boksutgivning. PRINT PDF används främst för särskilda tryckflöden eller lokal utskrift, medan DOCX ofta används för plattformar som Amazon KDP. Därför behövs det inte alltid i första steget.

Om du redigerar manuset i Microsoft Word, LibreOffice eller en annan editor kan dokumentstrukturen ändras. Spara filen igen som .docx och kör Re-Export i BOLO för att bygga upp den interna strukturen på nytt och förbättra kompatibiliteten med publiceringsplattformar som Amazon KDP.

Obs (LibreOffice / OpenOffice): Om du inte använder Microsoft Word kan du ändå redigera manuset. Öppna DOCX-filen i LibreOffice eller ett liknande verktyg, gör dina ändringar, spara filen och kontrollera att den sparas som .docx. Använd därefter Re-Export i BOLO för att reparera och bygga upp dokumentstrukturen på nytt.

■11. Format och typiska användningsområden

Det här avsnittet är tänkt som en praktisk referens. Programmet visar exakta storlekar i millimeter och inches, men många användare förstår formaten snabbare när de jämförs med bekanta saker som en pocketbok, en vanlig sakprosabok, en arbetsbok eller en coffee table-bok.

BOLO innehåller följande bokformat: A5, A4, B5, Royal, Crown, Pocket, 152 × 229 mm (6 × 9 in), 127 × 203 mm (5 × 8 in), 178 × 254 mm (7 × 10 in) och 216 × 279 mm (8.5 × 11 in). Programmet innehåller också Standard presentation och Website overview i presentationsverktyget, samt flyergenerering som anpassar sig till olika flyertyper.

■Bokformat i korthet

Små handhållna format

Pocket · 110 × 178 mm · 127 × 203 mm (5 × 8 in)
Exempeltitel
Undertitel
  • Pocket känns som en liten paperback som du kan hålla i en hand.

A5 · 148 × 210 mm · 152 × 229 mm (6 × 9 in) · Royal
Exempeltitel
Undertitel
  • A5 känns som en vanlig mindre sakprosabok eller arbetsbok.
  • 6 × 9 in är ett av de mest typiska standardformaten för sakprosa.

Större arbetsboks- och illustrationsformat

B5 · Crown · 178 × 254 mm (7 × 10 in)
Exempeltitel
Undertitel
  • B5 fungerar bra för utbildningsböcker, illustrerade böcker och luftigare layouter.
  • Crown känns som ett större trade-format med mer visuell rymd.

Dokument- och rapportformat

A4 · 210 × 297 mm · 216 × 279 mm (8.5 × 11 in)
Exempeltitel
Undertitel
  • A4 känns som vanligt kontorspapper och är idealiskt för rapporter, manualer och undervisningsmaterial.

■Enkel formatlogik

Tänk “Pocket”

Litet, kompakt, lätt att hålla. Bra när du vill ha en äkta paperback-känsla.

Tänk “Normal bok”

A5, 6 × 9 och Royal är de säkraste valen för vanliga sakprosaböcker.

Tänk “Arbetsbok / rapport”

B5, 7 × 10, A4 och 8.5 × 11 är bäst när layoututrymme är viktigare än bokhandelskänsla.
